How it's made



The process starts by choosing the wood. The wood board is then sanded to a smooth finish. It is then washed with a high quality stain and coated with semi-gloss polyurethane. The image is then engraved with an industry leading laser. Next I meticulously add beautiful detail, by hand, using water based metallic acrylic paint. Everything is then sealed with a final coat of semi-gloss poly.
